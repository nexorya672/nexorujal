Pro Tips & Tricks

🎯 The 60-30-10 Rule

Use 60% dominant color, 30% secondary, 10% accent. This creates visual hierarchy without chaos.

⚡ Performance First

Optimize images with WebP format, lazy load below-fold content, and keep your bundle size under 200KB for instant loads.

📱 Mobile-First Mindset

Design for 375px width first, then scale up. 60%+ of traffic is mobile. Don't be that guy with a broken mobile site.

🔒 Security Basics

Always use HTTPS, sanitize user inputs, implement rate limiting, and never store passwords in plain text. Ever.

🎭 Micro-Interactions

Subtle hover effects, loading animations, and transition states make your site feel alive. Don't overdo it though.

🧪 A/B Test Everything

Your opinion doesn't matter. Data does. Test button colors, headlines, layouts. Let the numbers decide.
